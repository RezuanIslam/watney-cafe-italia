About Us

A little piece of Italy on Tarling Street

Watney Café Italia began as a small cafe and grew into a neighbourhood favourite — Italian café, everyday store. We roast, bake and stock with the same care, so you can grab your morning espresso and your evening shopping in one warm, welcoming stop.

  • Freshly ground Italian espresso, daily
  • Grocery essentials, bakery & gifts under one roof
  • Open every day, 8am – 11pm
  • Proudly part of the E1 community
